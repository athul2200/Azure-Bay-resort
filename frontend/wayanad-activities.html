Idyllically nestled amid the tranquil and verdant Western Ghats, towards the south of Deccan Plateau, Wayanad is an exotic tourist destination. A UNESCO world heritage, and a must-see destination for all nature enthusiasts, Wayanad is home to lush tropical forests, mystic mountains, and verdant tea gardens. An overwhelmingly beautiful location with endless display of greenery in opulence, Wayanad is a renowned tourist spot that attracts thousands of vacationers from all over the world.
