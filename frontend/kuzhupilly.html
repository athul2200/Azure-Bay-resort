Kuzhupilly Beach, Palakkad

Azure Bay

Azure Bay is a discreet little hideaway set on a stretch of land between the Cherai lagoon to the east and the Kuzhupilly beach to the west.A stone paved path winds past lush palms and a profusion of tropical plants, leading you to an unspoiled white sand beach. It's the perfect destination for those seeking privacy and serenity away from the crowds, where the only music is the sound of the waves.

Activities

Accommodation

Heritage Room

Experience the timeless charm of our Heritage Rooms. Traditionally designed with authentic architecture, these relaxing rooms offer a unique cultural stay near the beach.

Backwater Bungalow

Nestled along the serene backwaters, our Backwater Bungalows offer a tranquil escape. Wake up to the gentle ripples of water right from your private veranda.

Backwater View Room

Enjoy panoramic vistas from our Backwater View Rooms. Elegantly furnished, these rooms provide a perfect vantage point to take in the breathtaking beauty of lush surroundings.

Shoreline Cafe

Relax at our rustic Shoreline Cafe, where we serve light coastal snacks, fresh fruit juices, and organic salads.

It's the perfect spot to grab a healthy bite after a morning yoga session while enjoying the sea breeze under the shade of our thatched roof.

Beachside Deck

Our Beachside Deck is an open-air platform designed for intimate gatherings, acoustic music nights, and private beach dinners.

With the stars above and the sand beneath, it offers an unpretentious yet deeply romantic setting for your special moments.

Resort Facilities

Hammock Garden

Lush garden area with strategically placed hammocks for deep relaxation.

Beach Showers

Outdoor bamboo showers to refresh yourself after a dip in the sea.
