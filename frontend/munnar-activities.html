Adventures at Birds Valley

Immerse yourself in the tranquility and thrill of the Munnar highlands.

Back to Munnar Resort

Campfire with music

Music is the food of love. And that too with a campfire... Of course the very idea itself make more sense than words. Campfire with music surrounded by the flora and fauna of the jungle ,along with the chirping of night birds around you will take you to another world of fantasy. This is an ideal way to get out of your stressful life schedule. You can dance around , sing around or just sit watching the beauty of the night sky counting the stars. You can also add little spice to your campfire experience by having the specialty dishes served hot from the restaurant. Altogether this music with campfire will give you an experience of your life time at Birds valley resort.

River bathing

Birds valley resorts have given extra care to keep everything natural to help you to return to nature . Having a bath in Muthirappuzha river along with your family will take you to another world of joy where you and your children can spend a lot of time playing in the natural water. This is going to be a novel experience for all of you and of course a great escape from your artificial swimming pools. This novel idea of river bathing is an exclusive offer only from birds valley resorts, Munnar for its esteemed customers.

Birthday party

Nowadays birthdays have become a part of any family’s celebration time. It is time for a reunion with your friends and relatives. And of course these parties have become more demanding and creative with children playing a major role in deciding the theme and menu. Considering all these facts, We at birds valley organize birthday theme parties and exclusive family birthday parties to fulfil your kid’s wishes.

Poolside Barbecue

The area, featuring a poolside barbecue, is just perfect for hosting memorable pool side parties and dinners.
