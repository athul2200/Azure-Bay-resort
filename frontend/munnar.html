Azure Bay Birds Valley

Dawn breaks over the line of mountains to the east and in the growing warmth the eponymously named Birds Valley comes alive with the sounds of over 60 indigenous and transient species of birds.Spread over 6 acres, Azure Bay Birds Valley is an exquisite hideaway set on a slope of a hillock, designed to provide the warmth and comforts of a home and the luxury of a premium resort.

Activities

Accommodation

MOUNTAIN VIEW COTTAGE

Open your windows to the breathtaking view of the hills pillowed by the soft clouds glowing in the glory of the morning sun. The cottages at Birds Valley Resort offer a panoramic view of the surrounding hills along with the promise of a unique holiday experience.

HONEYMOON COTTAGE

Warm cottages with private terraces to enjoy the beauty of the splendid surroundings. You can even enjoy a candlelight dinner under the starry sky with your beloved partner. Honeymoon cottages come with all facilities of other cottages along with better privacy.

WOODEN COTTAGE

Experience the rustic charm of our wooden cottages, thoughtfully crafted to offer a cozy and intimate stay. Blending naturally with the serene surroundings, these cottages provide beautiful views and modern comforts.

Valley View Restaurant

Indulge in a culinary journey at our signature restaurant, where organic ingredients meet Munnar's traditional spices.

Enjoy multi-cuisine delicacies while overlooking the mist-covered valleys. Our farm-to-table approach ensures freshness in every bite.

Misty Hall

Our elegant banquet hall provides the perfect setting for corporate retreats, private celebrations, and intimate weddings.

Equipped with modern audiovisual facilities and supported by our impeccable service, Misty Hall ensures your events are as memorable as the landscape around them.
