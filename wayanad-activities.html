Western Ghats Expeditions

Unveil the wild secrets of Wayanad's ancient rainforests.

Back to Wayanad Resort

Guided Night Safaris

Experience the rainforest like never before. As the sun sets, the jungle transforms. Embark on a thrilling 4x4 safari through the buffer zones of the Wayanad Wildlife Sanctuary. Led by certified forest rangers, you'll have the chance to spot nocturnal wildlife including leopard cats, civets, and if you're lucky, the elusive Malabar Giant Squirrel.

  • Duration: 2 Hours
  • Timings: 7:00 PM - 9:00 PM
  • Requirement: Prior booking required

Pozhuthana Bamboo Rafting

Glide silently down the crystal-clear waters of the Pozhuthana River on a traditional bamboo raft. This eco-friendly journey offers a unique perspective of the dense riverine vegetation and the majestic Chembra Peak in the background. It's a peaceful experience, perfect for bird watching and soaking in the serene sounds of nature.

  • Duration: 1.5 Hours
  • Highlights: River bathing & Birding
  • Inclusions: Life jackets & Guide

High-Altitude Forest Trekking

For the adventurer at heart, we offer guided treks to some of Wayanad's most breathtaking peaks and hidden waterfalls. Navigate through varying terrains, from open grasslands to dense shola forests. Our naturalists will share insights into the tribal folklore associated with these mountains and the medicinal importance of the local flora.

  • Difficulty: Moderate to Challenging
  • Route: Custom based on fitness level
  • Essentials: Sturdy footwear & Water bottle

Indigenous Culture Tours

Wayanad is home to ancient tribes with rich, untouched traditions. Our cultural interaction program takes you to the Paniya and Kuruma settlements in a respectful and non-intrusive manner. Witness their unique agricultural practices, traditional architecture, and deep-rooted connection with the forest ecosystem.

  • Focus: Sustainable living & Tribal art
  • Ethical Note: Respectful attire required
  • Inclusions: Local translator guide
